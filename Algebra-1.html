Algebra 1


Concept Description
Foundational Algebraic Skills Algebra, is built on critical thinking and step-by-step solving of equations. To understand these concepts you need to learn: how to use variables, order of operations, evaluating basic expressions, properties of numbers and arithmetical functions, and how to simplify expressions.
Solving Algebraic Equations To solve, and understand algebraic equations you need to understand basic 1-2 step equations and multi-step equations(3+), multi-variable equation solving, rewriting and solving formulae, and absolute values.
Inequalities Inequalities require several skills to understand and master: how to graph inequalities, solving 1+ step inequalities, compound inequalities, and absolute value inequalities.
Functions Functions are an element of Algebra 1 seen throughout different levels of maths and is comprised of: definition of a function, function notation, domain and range, linear vs. nonlinear, graphing and evaluating functions.
Linear Equations Linear Equations are one of the key concepts of Algebra 1. To understand and master this key concept, you need to know: how to find slope, slope-intercept form: y=mx+b, point-slope form: y1-y2 = m(x1-x2), standard form: Ax+Bx=C, and how to graph linear equations.
Systems of Equations Systems of Equations are how we solve multi-variable equations. You solve these equations by graphing, substitution, and elimination. To fully understaned this algebraic concept you need to learn their applications.
Exponents and Powers Exponents and Powers are basic algebraic concepts seen in earlier mathematical classes, but get more complex at the algebra level: law of exponents, product rule, quotient rule, exponential properties, negative and zero Exponents, and scientific notation.
Polynomials Polynomials are a type of equation that require specfic skills to solve: understanding how to add and subtract polynomials, FOIL method, Special Products, Polynomial division, and how to factor.
Quadratic Equations Quadratic equations are a special type of equation that have many applications. To learn quadratic equations, you need to understand how to graph and factor quadratic functions, and solve, using completing squares, and quadratic formula.
Radical Expressions Radical Expressions apply and build off of concepts from Exponents and Powers they can viewed as the inverse of Exponents: Simplifying square roots, adding, subtracting, multiplying radicals, rationalizing denominators and solving radical equations.
Basic Statistics Intro into basic mathematical statistics: Mean/median/mode, probability, scatter plots and correlation, and line of best fit.
